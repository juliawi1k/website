> Brains

Getting Into Worms' Brains - My Final Year Project (7 min): Overview of my final year project in which I've analysed recordings of brains of worms in order to identify neurons responsible for heat avoidance behaviour. Includes links to the full report and the code.

Hacking Brains: The Security Of Brain-Computer Interfaces (16 min): My talk from the BSides London 2022 conference. Topics include: recently identified vulnerabilities, potential attack vectors, available products and technology, medical devices, privacy concerns, and what makes security difficult in BCIs.


> Malware
